Metallica

Master Of Puppets

History

Released on March 3, 1986 by Elektra record, it was the band's last album to feature bassist Cliff Burton, who died in a bus crash in Sweden during the album's promotional tour. Master of Puppets topped at number 29 on the billboard 100 charts and received wide spread praise and recognition from both critics and fans alike.
